Venice Grand Canal Mall

Venice Grand Canal Mall is a lifestyle center located in McKinley Hill Taguig City, Philippines. The mall is inspired by the romantic city of Venice in Italy and features a replica of the Grand Canal, where visitors can ride on gondolas with singing gondoliers.

Market Market

Market! Market!, located in Bonifacio Global City, Manila, is a unique blend of traditional Filipino marketplace and modern shopping mall, offering a wide variety of affordable retail, dining, and entertainment options in a vibrant, bustling atmosphere.

The Mind Museum

The Mind Museum is a well-anticipated, world class Science museum in the Philippines. Log on to know more about the amazing science galleries and exhibits.

SM Aura

SM Aura, is a large upscale shopping mall located at McKinley Parkway corner 26th Street, Bonifacio Global City, Taguig, Philippines, owned by SM Prime Holdings, the country's largest mall developer. It is the 13th SM Supermall in Metro Manila and 47th SM Prime mall in the Philippines. 

Bonifacio High Street

This pedestrian-friendly street is lined with restaurants, shops, and cafes. It's a great place to relax, people-watch, and enjoy the city atmosphere.

Manila American Cementery and Memorial

The Manila American Cemetery and Memorial is a military cemetery located in Fort Bonifacio, Taguig. It can be reached most easily from the city via EDSA to McKinley Road, then to McKinley Parkway inside the Bonifacio Global City.

Fort Santiago

Fort Santiago, built in 1571, is a citadel built by Spanish navigator and governor Miguel López de Legazpi for the newly established city of Manila in the Philippines.

St. Anne Parish Church

This Baroque-style church is one of the oldest in Taguig. It's a great place to learn about the city's history and culture.

BGC Arts Center

The BGC Arts Center is an internationally acclaimed performing arts theater located in the heart of Manila. It regularly hosts world-renowned performers and has a wide range of shows to choose from, catering to any budget.

Uptown Mall

This upscale mall is home to a variety of luxury brands, as well as restaurants, cafes, and a cinema.

Active Fun Bonifacio High Street

This amusement park is perfect for a day of fun with family or friends. It features a variety of rides, games, and attractions.

Serendra Global Art Center

This art center houses a variety of galleries showcasing contemporary Filipino and international art. It's a great place to see some of the best art in the Philippines.
