The most visited places in Taguig City

Venice Grand Canal Mall

Venice Grand Canal Mall is a lifestyle center located in McKinley Hill Taguig City, Philippines. The mall is inspired by the romantic city of Venice in Italy and features a replica of the Grand Canal, where visitors can ride on gondolas with singing gondoliers.

Market Market

Market! Market!, located in Bonifacio Global City, Manila, is a unique blend of traditional Filipino marketplace and modern shopping mall, offering a wide variety of affordable retail, dining, and entertainment options in a vibrant, bustling atmosphere.

The Mind Museum

The Mind Museum is a well-anticipated, world class Science museum in the Philippines. Log on to know more about the amazing science galleries and exhibits.
